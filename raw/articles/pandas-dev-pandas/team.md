## Diversity and Inclusion

> _pandas_ expressly welcomes and encourages contributions from anyone who faces under-representation, discrimination in the technology industry or anyone willing to increase the diversity of our team.
> We have a code of conduct to ensure a friendly and welcoming environment.
> Please send an email to pandas-coc@googlegroups.com if you think we can do a better job at achieving this goal.
