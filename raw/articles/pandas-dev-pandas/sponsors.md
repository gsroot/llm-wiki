# Sponsors

## NumFOCUS

_pandas_ is a Sponsored Project of [NumFOCUS](https://numfocus.org/), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit charity in the United States.
NumFOCUS provides _pandas_ with fiscal, legal, and administrative support to help ensure the
health and sustainability of the project. Donations to _pandas_ are managed by NumFOCUS — for donors
in the United States, gifts are tax-deductible to the extent provided by law.

## Become a sponsor

As a free and open source project, _pandas_ relies on the support of the community of users for its development.
If you work for an organization that uses and benefits from _pandas_, please consider supporting pandas — by
employing people to work on pandas, funding the project, or becoming a NumFOCUS sponsor to support the broader
ecosystem. Contact admin@numfocus.org to discuss.
