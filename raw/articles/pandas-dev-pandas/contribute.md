# Contribute to pandas

_pandas_ is and will always be **free**. To make the development sustainable, we need _pandas_ users, corporate
and individual, to support the development by providing their time and money.

## Three contribution modes

### Corporate support

pandas depends on companies and institutions using the software to support its development. Hiring people to work
on pandas, or letting existing employees contribute to the software. Or sponsoring pandas with funds, so the project
can hire people to progress on the pandas roadmap. (More info in the sponsors page.)

### Individual contributors

pandas is mostly developed by volunteers. All kind of contributions are welcome — code, website (including graphical
designers), documentation (including translators), and others. There are tasks for all levels, including beginners.
(More info in the contributing page.)

### Donations

Individual donations are appreciated, used for project infrastructure, travel expenses for volunteer contributors
to attend in-person sprints, or to give small grants to develop features. Donate at: https://opencollective.com/pandas
